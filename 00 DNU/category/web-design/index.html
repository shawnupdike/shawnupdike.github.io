January 5, 2023
by updikes@ymail.com Web Design

Starting From Scratch...

Problem:

Develop a brand image and online platform where partnering school districts can have their students make an online account and trade items with other students from other districts in which Fruit Basket fulfills the delivery of said items to the student. Setup each students account to be customizable with their name, school and create a carrot reward system that teachers can use to incentive students to do better in their classes. In short, an e-commerce platform without any exchange of currency and strictly have it setup like an online bartering system.

Bon Appetite

Solution:

Fruit Basket came together in a very non-linear fashion but it got built from the ground up with nothing more than a vision. The execution of the project took about two months to erect but had a successful conclusion. It stands as one of my most challenging endeavors to date.

Groundwork

Process:

With a very specific ask, I knew I would have to approach this project from a much less traditional angle. Owner, Donald White came to me with a vision of a brand and online forum that would allow students to online trade. Producing the foundation his brand in Illustrator and hosting and building his website through WordPress, the groundwork was laid down to really get the real work going. Donald and I would meet routinely to discuss and strategize the structure of the website and how he wanted it to function. There were several setbacks but they came with solutions.

Smaht Weather Mobile App

April 11, 2021
by updikes@ymail.com with No Comment Web Design

Wicked Smaht!

Problem:

Develop a user interface that will give access to the weather in the Greater New England area, but also incorporate a branding image behind it that makes the user want to keep coming back to use the app to check the weather.

Out of the pahk!

Solution:

User testing concluded & improvements were made from feedback to enhance the app to be better for all users. The subtle brand humor was enjoyed & the app came together with excellent functionality, easy navigation & aspect ratio responsiveness.

Data Driven

Process:

I started with assembling a strong, trusting & professional branding image with a hint of New England verbiage humor to keep the user engage in a simple weather app. Sketches & wireframes were created, tested & used actual live weather data from an API. Smaht Weather is a real live working app that was built in HTML, CSS & Sass, pulled data from DarkSky API, animated and brought to life through After Effects and Javascript.
