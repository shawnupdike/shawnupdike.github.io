Back To Basics

Problem:

Develop e-commerce site with an existing brand identity that sells antiques, art, music, media & more. Client is expecting a modern & clean site design with an active dark theme that brings out the brand message. RSA aims to bring old school vintage items to the modern world, selling the past with a revamped taste.

Picture Perfect

Solution:

RSA e-commerce project was a successful outcome, the client was more than please from start to finish. With the site handed off, the intention was supposed to make the back end as user friendly as possible. As is the great problem to solve when making web applications for others.

Blank Canvas

Process:

After hosting was setup, I went straight to work assembling their photography assets shot by me. Photos of featured products that would be sold on the site. Woo-commerce was utilized to build their product listing pages. Utilizing WordPress, I began to take their existing brand standards and put them into action on their site. Using muted greys and black accents, the dark theme was enacted.
